About Us

New Life Indian Church is a welcoming family where you can discover a deeper relationship with Jesus, grow in community, and serve the world around you. Our Sunday services blend heartfelt worship, Biblical teaching, and genuine fellowship.

We’d love to meet you—join us this weekend or connect with one of our ministries throughout the week.
