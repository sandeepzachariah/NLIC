Welcome to New Life Indian Church

A community of faith, hope, and love serving Pittsburgh’s Indian and global families.

Join Us Sunday

We're Glad You're Here

We’re a gospel-centered, family-friendly church where you can know Jesus, grow in Scripture, and find a welcoming community.

Services blend heartfelt worship (English, Malayalam & Telugu expressions), Bible teaching, and prayer. There’s a place for you—students, families, and newcomers.

What we believe

Our Story

Following God’s call, our pastor’s family moved to Pittsburgh in June 2013, with our first service on September 21, 2014. We’ve grown as a home for immigrants, students, and families seeking Christ.

Vision & Mission

To see people transformed by Jesus and planted in a life-giving church. We proclaim the gospel, disciple believers, strengthen families, and serve our city through prayer, care, and outreach.
